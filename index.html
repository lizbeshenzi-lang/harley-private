/ 001 — The practice

A GP surgery that behaves like one

Harley Private was founded in 2015 by a group of NHS consultants who believed that private medicine had lost its way — spending more energy on marble lobbies than on clinical outcomes. We stripped it back.

You see the same doctor every time. Your appointments run to time. If you need a specialist, we have the relationships to get you a real appointment in 24 hours, not a six-week wait. That is private medicine as it should work.
